News Video Generator — Case Study

Problem → Auto-generate news videos Role → Prototyper Tools → Python, Automation Outcome → Pipeline concept validated

Problem

Automating the transformation of news content into short-form videos with simple narration and stock footage clips.

Your Role

Built the early prototype pipeline: sourcing content, arranging clips, adding audio, and exporting basic drafts.

Tools Used

  • Python scripts and automation
  • FFmpeg or similar video processing tools
  • GitHub for versioning

Outcome

Validated feasibility and identified bottlenecks for scaling. Next steps: template system and asset management.
